# Industry Report Skill

## Purpose
Generate a structured industry research report for a given topic and time range.

## Workflow
1. Collect industry signals from search and trusted sources.
2. Cluster signals into policy, market, competitor, risk, and technology categories.
3. Summarize the most important developments.
4. Produce a concise but structured report.

## Output Requirements
- Core conclusions
- Key developments
- Trend judgment
- Risk warning
- Recommended actions

## Best Practices
- Prefer recent and trustworthy sources.
- Avoid repeating duplicate signals.
- Preserve source links in the final artifact.
- Keep report tone concise and decision-oriented.
